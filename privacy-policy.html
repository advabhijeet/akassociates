Privacy Policy

This policy explains how information shared through this website may be received and handled by AK Associates.

Information Handling

This website is primarily informational. AK Associates may receive personal information when a visitor voluntarily contacts the chambers through phone, WhatsApp, email, or another communication method listed on the website.

Such information may include a visitor's name, phone number, email address, basic matter details, and documents or messages voluntarily shared for communication purposes.

Information shared through this website is used only to understand and respond to the communication. Visitors should avoid sending confidential or sensitive documents until a formal consultation or engagement is confirmed.

AK Associates does not sell visitor information. Information may be retained as required for communication records, professional obligations, or applicable legal requirements.

This website may contain links to third-party services such as WhatsApp or email clients. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies.

For privacy-related communication, visitors may write to kadvocateabhijeet@gmail.com.
