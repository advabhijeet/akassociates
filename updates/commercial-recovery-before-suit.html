Commercial Recovery

Before Filing A Commercial Recovery Suit

A practical checklist for businesses organizing contracts, invoices, account records and pre-filing steps before a recovery action.

Informational Note

Start With The Contract

Before any recovery action, collect the agreement, purchase order, work order, invoice terms, jurisdiction clause, arbitration clause, payment schedule and amendments or email confirmations.

Build The Money Trail

A recovery file should show invoice-wise dues, part payments, ledger balance, bank records, delivery or completion proof, debit notes, credit notes and the latest acknowledgement or dispute.

Check Pre-Filing Issues

Commercial disputes may require forum, specified value, limitation, urgent relief and pre-institution mediation questions to be checked before choosing the next procedural step.
