# Legal Monitoring Output Template

This document provides a reusable template for recording legal developments discovered during monitoring before deciding whether to draft a Chambers of AK article.

Use this together with:

```text
docs/planning/LEGAL_SOURCE_WATCHLIST.md
docs/planning/LEGAL_MONITORING_AND_ORIGINAL_ARTICLE_WORKFLOW.md
docs/planning/CONTENT_CALENDAR_AND_TOPIC_QUEUE.md
docs/maintenance/ARTICLE_PUBLISHING_WORKFLOW.md
```

## Purpose

A monitoring scan should not directly become a published article. First, each legal development should be recorded, verified, scored and reviewed.

This template helps maintain:

- source discipline;
- primary-source verification;
- originality;
- practice-area relevance;
- non-solicitation language;
- a clean audit trail for future articles.

## Relevance Score Guide

```text
5 - Directly affects a core practice area and is useful for readers now.
4 - Strongly relevant and suitable for an explanatory article after verification.
3 - Some relevance, but may be better for content calendar or future monitoring.
2 - Background information only.
1 - Not relevant for Chambers of AK publication.
```

## Recommended Action Guide

### Reject

Use when:

- source is unreliable;
- topic is irrelevant;
- no legal/practical value for the website;
- too speculative or political;
- cannot be verified.

### Watch

Use when:

- development is proposed or pending;
- more information is expected;
- primary source is not yet available;
- issue may become relevant later.

### Shortlist

Use when:

- topic is relevant;
- source appears reliable;
- article angle is useful;
- primary verification is still needed.

### Verify Further

Use when:

- secondary legal news reports a judgment or amendment;
- official judgment/order/notification must be located;
- status is unclear.

### Draft Article

Use when:

- source is verified;
- relevance score is 4 or 5;
- article can be written originally;
- internal linking opportunity exists;
- topic is suitable for Chambers of AK audience.

### Add To Content Calendar

Use when:

- topic is useful but not urgent;
- it supports long-term SEO clusters;
- it can be planned for a monthly batch.

### Update Existing Page

Use when:

- an existing article/practice/service page should be refreshed instead of creating a new page;
- the development affects existing content accuracy;
- only a small update is needed.

## Article Conversion Checklist

Before converting a monitored item into an article, confirm:

```text
[ ] Primary or official source checked where available.
[ ] Legal status understood: final/proposed/stayed/appealed/etc.
[ ] Topic is relevant to a core practice area.
[ ] Original article angle identified.
[ ] No copying or close paraphrasing of third-party article text.
[ ] Internal links identified.
[ ] Official/source references identified.
[ ] Non-solicitation language can be maintained.
[ ] Article slug selected.
[ ] Social distribution angle identified.
```
