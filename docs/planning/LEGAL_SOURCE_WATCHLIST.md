## Purpose

Create a reliable source watchlist for identifying legal developments, judgments, notifications, bills, circulars and trending legal issues that may be converted into original Chambers of AK legal insight articles.

This watchlist is for discovery and verification. It is not permission to copy or republish third-party articles.

## Verification Rule

Before publishing an article based on a development:

1. Identify whether the source is primary, official or secondary.
2. Prefer primary/official sources wherever available.
3. Use secondary legal news sources only for discovery unless no primary source is accessible.
4. Confirm whether a development is final, proposed, pending, stayed, appealed, notified or only reported.
5. Write a fresh original Chambers of AK article in the firm's own words.
6. Include official/source references where appropriate.
7. Preserve informational and non-solicitation language.

## Source Priority Levels

```text
Priority 1: Primary / official source
Priority 2: Regulator / government department source
Priority 3: Court or tribunal portal source
Priority 4: Secondary legal news discovery source
Priority 5: General news discovery source
```

Only Priority 1 to 3 sources should normally be relied on for legal accuracy. Priority 4 and 5 sources may be used to discover topics, then verify from Priority 1 to 3 sources.

## Monitoring Frequency

Recommended schedule:

### Weekly

- Supreme Court judgments / legal updates.
- Patna High Court updates.
- Ministry/Gazette updates for practice clusters.
- MSME, RERA and RBI updates.
- Secondary legal news discovery scan.

### Monthly

- Search Console query review.
- Topic queue refresh.
- Feed/article pipeline review.
- Service-page opportunity review.

### Event-Based

Monitor immediately when there is:

- major Supreme Court judgment;
- amendment bill or notified amendment;
- new Gazette notification affecting practice areas;
- RERA/MSME/RBI regulatory update;
- major procedural rule change.

## Article Conversion Rule

A monitored item should become a Chambers of AK article only if:

- it is relevant to a core practice area;
- it can be verified from a reliable source;
- it can be explained in original words;
- it benefits readers through practical, document-first explanation;
- it can be linked to existing practice or service pages;
- it preserves non-solicitation language.

## First Monitoring Topics To Look For

Priority monitoring topics:

- new Supreme Court / High Court rulings on Section 138 NI Act;
- MSME delayed-payment and MSEFC developments;
- RERA delayed possession, refund or enforcement developments;
- arbitration Section 9, Section 11, Section 34 or enforcement developments;
- SARFAESI/DRT borrower-rights or lender-recovery developments;
- property title, mutation, registration or land-acquisition notifications;
- commercial court procedure or limitation developments;
- trademark/IP procedural updates.

## Important Reminder

This watchlist supports article discovery. It does not authorize automated publication.

All final articles should be original, reviewed and published through the standard article workflow.
