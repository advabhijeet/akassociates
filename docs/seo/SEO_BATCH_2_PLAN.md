## Page Quality Rules

Every Batch 2 public page must include:

- unique SEO title;
- unique meta description;
- canonical URL;
- Open Graph metadata;
- Twitter card metadata;
- appropriate JSON-LD, usually `Article`, `FAQPage`, or `WebPage`;
- internal links to related practice, service, update and enquiry pages;
- WhatsApp and case-enquiry CTAs;
- informational/non-solicitation disclaimer language;
- no outcome guarantees;
- no wording that implies automatic acceptance of every matter or forum appearance.

## Internal Linking Rules

Each service page should link to:

- relevant practice page;
- at least one related existing service page;
- at least one related legal update article;
- `../case-enquiry.html`;
- `../document-checklists.html`, if relevant.

Each update article should link to:

- relevant practice page;
- one relevant service page;
- one related update article;
- `../case-enquiry.html`;
- `../document-checklists.html`, if relevant.
