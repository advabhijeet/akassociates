Case Enquiry

Use this page as a simple guide for the basic details that may be useful when making an initial enquiry about a legal matter.

Details To Share

Matter Type

Mention whether the matter relates to cheque bounce, MSME payment, RERA, arbitration, property, commercial recovery, or another issue.

Important Dates

Write down notice dates, cheque return date, payment due date, possession date, hearing date, order date, or limitation-sensitive dates.

Current Stage

State whether the matter is pre-litigation, notice stage, already filed, pending before a court/forum, or at execution/enforcement stage.

Useful Format

A short first message may include your name, city, matter type, opposite party name, important dates, forum or court if pending, and a brief summary of what has happened so far.

Please avoid sending confidential or sensitive documents until a formal consultation or engagement is confirmed. Initial communication through this website, email, phone, or WhatsApp does not create an advocate-client relationship.
