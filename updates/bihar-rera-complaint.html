Builder ne possession nahi diya? RERA complaint kaise karein

A general note on documents, reliefs, and basic procedural thinking in builder-buyer disputes before RERA authorities across Bihar, UP and Delhi NCR.

Informational Note

Start With The Agreement

The builder-buyer agreement, allotment letter, payment schedule, possession date, project registration details, and receipts should be reviewed before any complaint is drafted.

Identify The Relief

Common issues include delayed possession, refund, interest, incomplete amenities, cancellation, and enforcement of commitments. The relief sought should match the documents and legal route.

Choose The Correct RERA Authority

RERA complaints are normally connected to the project location and the applicable authority. Bihar is the chambers' homebase, while RERA and builder-dispute work may involve Bihar, UP and Delhi NCR depending on the property and documents.

Prepare The Record

A clean document set can make the procedural journey easier. Keep payment proofs, communications, project brochures, site updates, legal notices, and any prior complaint records together.

Documents Often Needed

  • Builder-buyer agreement or allotment letter.
  • Receipts, bank statements, and payment schedule.
  • Project details and promised possession timeline.
  • Email, message, notice, and reply records.
