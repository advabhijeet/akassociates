About

AK Associates is the chambers of Abhijeet Kumar, an advocate based in Patna, practising across Bihar, Delhi, and Uttar Pradesh. The chambers focuses on helping small and medium enterprises, traders, homebuyers, and property owners resolve disputes efficiently — whether through negotiation, arbitration, or determined litigation.

Abhijeet Kumar completed his legal education at Amity University, Noida and has been enrolled with the Bar Council of Bihar since 2022. He advises clients in both Hindi and English, maintaining direct communication at every stage of a matter.

If you have a matter involving a bounced cheque, stuck MSME payment, builder dispute, property suit, or commercial recovery — get in touch for a consultation.
