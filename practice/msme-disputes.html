Practice Area

MSME Delayed Payment Disputes

General information on payment recovery for micro and small enterprises, including claim preparation, Facilitation Council proceedings, conciliation, arbitration, and enforcement issues.

Matter Flow

Eligibility & Records

Udyam registration, purchase orders, invoices, delivery proof, ledger records, and communications are reviewed before any claim is prepared.

Council Proceedings

Proceedings may involve conciliation and, where required, arbitration under the MSMED framework before the appropriate council.

Enforcement

Orders or awards may require further steps for challenge, execution, or recovery depending on the outcome and forum.

Common Documents

Useful documents may include Udyam registration details, invoices, purchase orders, delivery challans, email or WhatsApp communications, ledger statements, payment reminders, and acknowledgement of dues.

This page is informational only and should not be treated as legal advice for any particular dispute.

Checklist

MSME Records

  • Udyam registration certificate/details.
  • GST details, if relevant.
  • Business correspondence.
  • Claim amount summary.

Transaction Papers

  • Purchase orders or work orders.
  • Invoices and delivery challans.
  • Ledger statement.
  • Bank payment records.

Recovery Record

  • Payment reminders.
  • Email or WhatsApp acknowledgement.
  • Legal notice, if any.
  • Any council filing or order.
