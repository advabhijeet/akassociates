Courts, Forums & Practice Locations

This page explains the courts, statutory forums and arbitral forums that may become relevant in MSME recovery, arbitration, commercial/consumer recovery, RERA/property and cheque bounce matters handled through Chambers of AK.

Court And Forum Assessment

The primary practice focus is Patna High Court, District Courts and forums of Bihar. Selected matters may involve Delhi courts, the Supreme Court of India, Noida/Ghaziabad forums or Allahabad High Court depending on jurisdiction, documents, stage and professional feasibility. This page is informational and does not promise appearance or outcome in any forum.

For first communication, share the matter type, city, court or forum if already pending, case number if available, order date, next hearing date and urgent relief requirement.

Key Forums

Patna High Court

Primary focus for writ, appellate, revisional, arbitration-related, civil-commercial and supervisory matters depending on facts, forum history and maintainability.

District Courts In Bihar

Primary focus for civil suits, injunctions, cheque bounce complaints, commercial recovery, consumer-linked disputes, execution and appeals.

Bihar Statutory Forums

Bihar-linked MSME, RERA, consumer, commercial and execution forums may become relevant depending on statutory route, documents and relief.

MSME Facilitation Council

Delayed-payment claims involving micro and small enterprises may require assessment of Udyam status, invoices, delivery records, buyer objections and statutory forum requirements.

Arbitral Tribunals

Contractual disputes may proceed before an arbitral tribunal when the contract contains an arbitration clause and procedural requirements are satisfied.

Bihar RERA

Builder-buyer disputes connected with Bihar projects may involve delayed possession, refund, interest, compensation or enforcement, subject to project documents and forum requirements.

Delhi Courts And Forums

Selected Delhi matters may be considered depending on jurisdiction, contract, parties, procedural stage and professional feasibility.

Supreme Court Of India

Selected matters may involve Supreme Court work depending on procedural stage, maintainability, record and professional feasibility.

Noida, Ghaziabad And Allahabad High Court

UP/NCR positioning is limited to Noida, Ghaziabad and Allahabad High Court-linked matters. Broad UP positioning is intentionally avoided.

Working Regions And Forum Boundaries

Primary: Patna And Bihar

The chambers is based in Patna. Patna High Court, District Courts and forums of Bihar are the primary practice and website-positioning focus.

Secondary: Delhi And Supreme Court

Selected Delhi court/forum matters and Supreme Court matters may be considered depending on jurisdiction, stage, documents and feasibility.

Limited UP/NCR Scope

UP/NCR positioning is limited to Noida, Ghaziabad and Allahabad High Court-linked matters. Forum selection depends on project location, contract terms, parties, cause of action and prior proceedings.

No Forum Guarantee

A forum cannot be chosen only from a short description. Jurisdiction, limitation, valuation, statutory pre-conditions, parties and relief must be reviewed before any formal step is advised.

Forum Route Checklist

Before Filing

Check the correct party names, address, territorial facts, valuation, limitation, statutory notice requirement, arbitration clause and whether an alternate forum is already seized of the dispute.

Pending Matters

If a matter is already pending, keep the case number, court/forum name, last order, next date, pleadings, notices, interim orders and urgent relief requirement ready.

After Orders / Awards

Orders, decrees, arbitral awards, RERA directions and settlement terms may need compliance, challenge, execution or enforcement review depending on the forum and limitation.

Practice Links

Relevant practice areas include MSME recovery, arbitration, commercial/consumer recovery, RERA/property disputes, and cheque bounce.
