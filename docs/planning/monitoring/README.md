# Legal Monitoring Batches

This folder stores internal legal monitoring batch notes for Chambers of AK.

Use these files to record legal developments discovered from courts, government sources, regulators, legal news discovery sources and other reliable references before deciding whether to create a public article.

## Purpose

Monitoring notes are internal planning documents. They are not public legal articles and should not be treated as final legal analysis.

Each monitoring batch should:

- record sources checked;
- identify relevant legal developments;
- verify from primary or official sources where possible;
- score relevance;
- recommend whether to reject, watch, shortlist, verify further, draft an article or update an existing page;
- preserve originality and non-solicitation standards.

## File Naming Convention

Use:

```text
YYYY-MM-DD_LEGAL_MONITORING_BATCH.md
```

Example:

```text
2026-05-07_LEGAL_MONITORING_BATCH.md
```

## Publication Rule

Do not publish a monitoring item directly.

Before publication:

1. Verify legal status and source.
2. Draft an original Chambers of AK article.
3. Use the article template.
4. Add legal-updates card, sitemap entry and feed item.
5. Prepare LinkedIn, WhatsApp Channel and newsletter drafts.
6. Request Search Console indexing.
7. Record validation and live-check status.
