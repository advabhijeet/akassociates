Before You Write

Please avoid sending confidential documents until a formal consultation or engagement is confirmed. For an initial query, it is usually sufficient to mention the nature of the matter, relevant dates, city or project location, forum or court if already pending, and whether any notice or order has been received.

For a structured list of details that may be useful in an initial message, see the case enquiry guide.
