About The Firm

Chambers of AK

Chambers of AK is a document-first legal chambers built for civil, commercial, recovery, property and regulatory-linked disputes, with a focused approach to pleadings, evidence, limitation, forum strategy and practical case preparation.

Firm Profile

Chambers of AK assists individuals, businesses, homebuyers, lenders, MSMEs and property owners in matters where documents, dates and procedural discipline determine the legal route.

The chambers works across civil litigation, property and title-related disputes, real estate due diligence, banking and DRT recovery proceedings, SARFAESI-linked matters, cheque dishonour cases, MSME delayed-payment recovery, RERA and builder disputes, arbitration, commercial contracts, lease arrangements, settlement deeds, wills, powers of attorney and selected intellectual property advisory.

The team has expertise in title search reports, property title verification, real estate transaction due diligence, DRT and debt recovery matters for financial institutions, NHAI-linked land acquisition and reimbursement disputes, commercial drafting, MSME recovery, arbitration and Negotiable Instruments Act proceedings.

Every enquiry is assessed in a document-led manner: first the papers, then the limitation and forum position, then the practical procedural route. This website is for general information only and is not intended as advertising, solicitation or legal advice.

Core Strengths

Real Estate, Title & Civil Property

Title search, due diligence, title verification, property documents, possession disputes, land acquisition-linked issues, injunctions, partition and civil property remedies.

Banking, DRT & Recovery

Document-led support in DRT proceedings, debt recovery, SARFAESI-linked matters, commercial dues, settlement records and evidence preparation.

MSME, Arbitration & NI Act

MSME delayed-payment disputes, arbitral proceedings, contract enforcement, award execution and cheque bounce matters under Section 138 NI Act.

Additional Expertise

Contracts & Private Documentation

Commercial contracts, lease agreements, settlement deeds, wills, powers of attorney and document review for transaction or dispute readiness.

Regulatory & Forum-Based Work

RERA complaints, MSME Facilitation Council matters, arbitral tribunals, DRT proceedings and court-stage litigation support where facts and forum permit.

Trademark & IP Advisory

Preliminary support for trademark registration, opposition-related issues and brand protection enquiries where legal review is required.

Approach

The chambers prioritises clear written instructions, indexed documents, date charts, realistic forum assessment and careful drafting. The objective is to reduce procedural confusion and help clients understand the next practical step before a matter is filed, defended, settled or escalated.

For a first review, use the case enquiry page and share only the necessary matter type, parties, dates, documents and current stage. Do not send confidential or privileged material until a formal engagement is confirmed.
