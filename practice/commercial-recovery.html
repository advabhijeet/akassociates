Practice Area

Commercial Recovery & Contract Disputes

General information on recovery suits, contractual disputes, injunction matters, summary proceedings where applicable, and civil or commercial court remedies.

Matter Flow

Claim Assessment

Contracts, invoices, ledgers, delivery proof, correspondence, and limitation dates are reviewed to assess the claim or defence.

Notice & Pleadings

Depending on the matter, steps may include legal notice, reply, plaint, written statement, interim application, or settlement discussion.

Court Proceedings

Procedural stages may include filing, service, interim relief, evidence, arguments, decree, appeal, or execution.

Common Documents

Useful documents may include contracts, purchase orders, invoices, ledger statements, delivery challans, emails, messages, notices, payment acknowledgements, and prior settlement records.

This page is informational only and does not replace advice based on documents and facts.

Checklist

Contract Papers

  • Agreement, purchase order or work order.
  • Terms and conditions.
  • Delivery or service scope.
  • Any amendment or extension.

Payment Record

  • Invoices and ledger statement.
  • Bank statement or payment proof.
  • Debit notes or credit notes.
  • Interest or claim calculation.

Dispute Trail

  • Emails and messages.
  • Legal notice and reply.
  • Acknowledgement of dues.
  • Settlement correspondence.
