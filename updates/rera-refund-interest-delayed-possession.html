Delayed Possession: Refund Or Interest?

A general note on thinking through relief in RERA and builder-buyer disputes where possession has been delayed.

Informational Note

Relief Depends On The Papers

Delayed possession disputes should begin with the allotment letter, builder-buyer agreement, payment plan, promised possession date, project registration details, receipts and all builder communications.

Refund, Interest Or Possession

Some allottees want possession with delay compensation. Others consider refund with interest. The appropriate relief depends on the agreement, delay, project status, payments made, communications and the forum route.

Forum Depends On Project Location

RERA forum selection is usually project-location based. Bihar is the chambers' homebase, while RERA and builder-dispute work may involve Bihar, UP and Delhi NCR depending on project details.

Useful Internal Pages

For a broader overview, see the RERA and builder disputes practice page and the focused page for a RERA lawyer for Bihar, UP and Delhi NCR.

Official Reference
