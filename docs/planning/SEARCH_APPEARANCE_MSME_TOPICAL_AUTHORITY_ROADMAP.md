NB Associates appears frequently for MSME-related queries because its site repeatedly reinforces:

- MSME payment / dues recovery;
- arbitration and alternate dispute resolution;
- statutory MSME Act topics;
- multiple MSME blog articles;
- service pages supported by informational pages;
- clear contact and location signals.

Chambers of AK should not copy their text or structure directly. The strategy is to compete with cleaner legal writing, better UX, updated legal accuracy, stronger Patna/Bihar positioning, and a document-first approach.

## Sitelink Readiness Plan

### Phase 1: Navigation Clarity

Primary navigation should consistently use:

- Home
- About
- Practice
- Enquiry
- Courts
- Insights
- Contact

Footer should repeat the most important links with concise anchor text.

### Phase 2: Hub Pages

Create or strengthen these hub pages:

- MSME Recovery
- Arbitration
- Commercial / Consumer Recovery
- RERA / Property
- Cheque Bounce

Each hub should include:

- overview;
- who the page is for;
- documents required;
- route options;
- limitation/date issues;
- forum/location notes;
- related articles;
- enquiry CTA;
- professional disclaimer note.

### Phase 3: Internal Linking

Rules:

- MSME articles link to MSME Recovery Hub.
- Arbitration articles link to Arbitration Hub.
- Section 34 articles link to Arbitration Hub and MSME Hub where relevant.
- Cheque bounce articles link to Cheque Bounce Hub.
- RERA articles link to RERA / Property Hub.
- Each hub links to 4–8 related articles.

### Phase 4: Schema

Use only appropriate structured data:

- Organization
- LegalService
- WebSite
- BreadcrumbList
- Article
- FAQPage only where the page genuinely contains FAQ content

Do not add fake reviews, unsupported rankings, or outcome guarantees.

### Phase 5: Sitemap Hygiene

Sitemap should include:

- homepage;
- important root pages;
- active practice hubs;
- active service pages;
- article pages;
- useful policy pages.

Sitemap should exclude:

- noindexed retired pages;
- preview/demo pages;
- duplicate pages;
- feed.xml;
- internal maintenance pages.

### Phase 6: Search Console Workflow

For every new major page or article:

1. update sitemap;
2. validate registry if article;
3. inspect URL in Google Search Console;
4. request indexing;
5. check coverage after 48–72 hours;
6. monitor impressions after 7–14 days.

## Guardrails

- Do not copy competitor text.
- Do not keyword-stuff.
- Do not claim guaranteed results.
- Do not overstate specialization.
- Do not create misleading location pages.
- Keep Patna/Bihar as the primary location signal.
- Keep Delhi/Supreme Court as secondary signal.
- Use Noida/Ghaziabad/Allahabad High Court only where contextually justified.
- Maintain non-solicitation and no advocate-client relationship disclaimers.
