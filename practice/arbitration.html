Practice Area

Arbitration

General information on domestic arbitration, including invocation notices, appointment issues, statement of claim, defence, evidence, hearings, interim relief, and award enforcement.

Matter Flow

Clause Review

The arbitration clause, governing law, seat, venue, appointment procedure, and notice requirements are reviewed first.

Pleadings & Evidence

Claims, counterclaims, defence, documents, witness material, and correspondence are organized for the arbitral record.

Award Stage

After an award, further steps may relate to enforcement, challenge, settlement, or execution depending on the outcome.

Common Documents

Useful documents may include contracts, arbitration clauses, purchase orders, invoices, correspondence, notices, account statements, performance records, minutes, and prior settlement communications.

This page is informational only. Arbitration strategy depends on the agreement, facts, and procedural stage.

Checklist

Agreement

  • Signed contract.
  • Arbitration clause.
  • Amendments or addendums.
  • Jurisdiction and seat details.

Claim Record

  • Invoices and account statement.
  • Performance or delivery proof.
  • Correspondence and minutes.
  • Loss or claim calculation.

Proceedings

  • Invocation notice.
  • Replies and appointment papers.
  • Pleadings or orders.
  • Award, if already passed.
