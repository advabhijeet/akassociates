Courts, Tribunals & Legal Forums

This page explains the courts, statutory forums and arbitral forums that may become relevant in civil, commercial, MSME, RERA, cheque bounce, property and arbitration matters handled through Chambers of AK.

Court And Forum Assessment

The appropriate court or forum depends on the cause of action, place of transaction, property location, contract clause, valuation, limitation, parties, relief sought and stage of the dispute. This page is informational and does not promise appearance or outcome in any forum.

For first communication, share the matter type, city, court or forum if already pending, case number if available, order date, next hearing date and urgent relief requirement.

Key Forums

Patna High Court

May become relevant for writ, appellate, revisional, civil-commercial, arbitration-related or supervisory matters depending on facts, forum history and maintainability.

District Courts in Bihar

Often relevant for civil suits, injunctions, property disputes, cheque bounce complaints, execution, appeals and other district-level proceedings.

Commercial Courts

May be relevant where a commercial dispute satisfies valuation and statutory requirements. Contracts, invoices, correspondence and pre-institution records should be preserved.

MSME Facilitation Council

Delayed-payment claims involving micro and small enterprises may require assessment of Udyam status, invoices, delivery records, buyer objections and statutory forum requirements.

Bihar RERA

Builder-buyer disputes connected with Bihar projects may involve delayed possession, refund, interest, compensation or enforcement, subject to project documents and forum requirements.

UP RERA / Delhi NCR Forums

For projects outside Bihar, project location and forum jurisdiction are critical. Allotment, payment, possession and builder communication records should be organized.

Arbitral Tribunals

Contractual disputes may proceed before an arbitral tribunal when the contract contains an arbitration clause and procedural requirements are satisfied.

Execution And Enforcement Forums

Orders, awards, decrees and settlement terms may require enforcement steps depending on the nature of the decision and the assets or parties involved.

Working Regions And Forum Boundaries

Patna And Bihar

The chambers is based in Patna. Bihar-linked matters may involve district courts, Patna High Court, Bihar RERA, commercial courts, authority proceedings, execution forums and other statutory routes depending on facts.

Delhi NCR / UP / Gurugram Context

Some enquiries may involve Delhi NCR, Noida, Gurugram, UP RERA, HRERA or Delhi-linked contracts. Forum selection depends on project location, contract terms, parties, cause of action and prior proceedings.

No Forum Guarantee

A forum cannot be chosen only from a short description. Jurisdiction, limitation, valuation, statutory pre-conditions, parties and relief must be reviewed before any formal step is advised.

Forum Route Checklist

Before Filing

Check the correct party names, address, territorial facts, valuation, limitation, statutory notice requirement, arbitration clause and whether an alternate forum is already seized of the dispute.

Pending Matters

If a matter is already pending, keep the case number, court/forum name, last order, next date, pleadings, notices, interim orders and urgent relief requirement ready.

After Orders / Awards

Orders, decrees, arbitral awards, RERA directions and settlement terms may need compliance, challenge, execution or enforcement review depending on the forum and limitation.

Practice Links

Relevant practice areas include cheque bounce, MSME disputes, RERA and builder disputes, commercial recovery, arbitration, and property and civil suits.
