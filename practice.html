Practice Readiness

Dates First

Most matters require a date chart before strategy can be assessed. Preserve notice dates, dishonour dates, invoice dates, possession timelines, limitation triggers, court dates and correspondence chronology.

Documents First

Complaints, replies, notices, agreements, ledgers, payment proofs, title papers, order sheets and official communications should be arranged in a clear indexed sequence before detailed review.

Forum First

The appropriate forum may depend on statute, contract, territorial facts, pecuniary value, project location, arbitration clause, court stage and the specific relief required.

Relief First

A matter should be assessed by the relief actually required: recovery, injunction, refund, possession, enforcement, defence, settlement, appeal, review of notices or document preparation.

Risk Review

Common risks include limitation, incomplete records, unclear liability, wrong forum, weak service proof, missing authorisation, unverified title papers and undocumented settlement terms.
