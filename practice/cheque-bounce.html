Cheque Bounce - Section 138

General information on cheque dishonour matters, including demand notice, limitation, complaint filing, defence, compounding, trial, and appeals under the NI Act.

Matter Flow

Initial Review

The cheque, return memo, transaction papers, and communications are reviewed to understand the debt or liability and limitation timeline.

Demand Notice

In many matters, timely statutory notice is a critical step. Dates of dishonour and receipt of information should be checked carefully.

Complaint Or Defence

Depending on the side represented, pleadings, documents, and evidence strategy are prepared for the competent court.

Common Documents

Useful documents may include the original cheque, bank return memo, invoices or loan papers, account statements, demand notice, postal receipts, delivery reports, replies, and any settlement communications.
