# Content Calendar And Topic Queue

This document records future Chambers of AK content priorities for legal insights, service pages and SEO growth.

## Purpose

Create a controlled content pipeline so future website updates are not random. Topics should be selected based on:

- search intent;
- relevance to Chambers of AK practice areas;
- document-first legal usefulness;
- Search Console impressions and query data;
- current legal developments;
- non-solicitation compliance.

## Publishing Frequency

Recommended minimum:

- 2 legal insight articles per month;
- 1 service/practice enhancement per month;
- 1 Search Console review per month.

Recommended stronger schedule when time permits:

- 1 legal insight article per week;
- 1 service/practice enhancement every 2 weeks;
- monthly content audit and indexing review.

## Monthly Content Review Checklist

At the beginning of each month:

1. Check Google Search Console:
   - pages gaining impressions;
   - pages with impressions but low CTR;
   - unindexed pages;
   - queries around practice areas.
2. Select 2 to 4 article topics.
3. Select 1 service/practice page to strengthen.
4. Check current legal developments for article opportunities.
5. Prioritize topics that support existing pages with internal links.

## Article Selection Criteria

Prefer topics that satisfy at least three of the following:

- connected to core practice area;
- likely searched by potential clients;
- document-first and practical;
- can be supported by official or primary sources;
- can internally link to an existing practice/service page;
- relevant to Bihar, Patna, Delhi NCR, UP, businesses, MSMEs, homebuyers or property owners;
- suitable for LinkedIn/WhatsApp Channel distribution.

Avoid topics that:

- require speculative legal predictions;
- rely only on another legal news website without primary source verification;
- are politically sensitive without clear legal relevance;
- invite direct solicitation;
- cannot be explained without legal advice specific to facts.
