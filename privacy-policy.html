Privacy Policy

This policy explains how information shared through this website may be received and handled by Chambers of AK.

Information Handling

Last updated: 10 May 2026

Scope

This Privacy Policy explains how Chambers of AK may receive, use, retain, and protect information shared through this website or through communication channels linked from this website.

This website is primarily informational. Visitors should read this policy together with the website disclaimer and terms and conditions.

Information voluntarily shared

Chambers of AK may receive personal information when a visitor voluntarily contacts the chambers through phone, WhatsApp, email, LinkedIn, the case enquiry flow, or another communication method listed on the website.

Such information may include a visitor's name, phone number, email address, location, basic matter details, preferred contact method, messages, and documents voluntarily shared for communication or consultation preparation.

Visitors should avoid sending confidential, privileged, highly sensitive, or original documents until a formal consultation or engagement is confirmed.

Purpose of use

Information shared by visitors may be used to understand the communication, respond to enquiries, schedule or prepare for consultations, conduct conflict checks, maintain communication records, comply with professional obligations, and comply with applicable legal requirements.

Chambers of AK does not sell visitor information.

Analytics and cookies

This website uses Google Tag Manager and Google Analytics 4 to understand basic website usage and important enquiry interactions, such as clicks on phone, email, WhatsApp, and case enquiry links.

Analytics tools may collect technical information such as device type, browser, approximate location, pages visited, time spent, referral source, and interaction events. These tools may use cookies or similar technologies controlled by Google and governed by Google's applicable policies.

The website may also include Google AdSense readiness files or scripts. If advertising is enabled after approval, ads are intended to appear only on legal update, case brief, checklist, guide, or article-style pages, and not on contact, enquiry, policy, or private-communication-focused pages.

Google AdSense and related advertising services may use cookies, web beacons, IP addresses, device identifiers, ad interaction data, and similar technologies to serve, measure, limit, or personalize ads according to Google's applicable policies and user controls. Visitors may manage certain Google advertising preferences through their Google account or browser settings.

If consent or additional notices are required for visitors in a particular region, the website may use Google's Privacy & messaging tools, a consent-management platform, or a separate cookie and advertising notice before advertising features are fully enabled.

Third-party platforms

This website may link to third-party services, including WhatsApp, LinkedIn, email clients, Google services, or other external platforms. When a visitor uses those services, their information is governed by the privacy policies and settings of the relevant third-party platform.

Retention and security

Information may be retained for as long as reasonably required for communication records, professional purposes, legal compliance, dispute resolution, or legitimate administrative needs.

Reasonable measures are taken to protect information received by Chambers of AK, but no website, email system, messaging service, or internet transmission can be guaranteed to be fully secure.

Children's information

This website is not directed at children. Minors should not submit personal information through this website without the involvement of a parent or lawful guardian.

Privacy requests

Visitors may request access, correction, deletion, or withdrawal of consent in relation to personal information voluntarily shared with Chambers of AK, subject to professional obligations, legal requirements, and legitimate retention needs.

For privacy-related communication, visitors may write to chambersofakadmin@gmail.com.

Policy updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect changes in website features, contact methods, analytics tools, legal requirements, or professional practices. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised date.
