About The Chambers

AK Associates

AK Associates is the chambers of Abhijeet Kumar, Advocate, based in Patna and engaged in dispute resolution, commercial recovery, property matters, and forum-based proceedings.

Profile
AdvocateAbhijeet Kumar
EducationLLB, Amity University, Noida
EnrollmentBar Council of Bihar, 2022
BasePatna, Bihar

AK Associates assists individuals, traders, small businesses, homebuyers, and property owners in matters that require careful legal assessment and procedural follow-through.

The chambers works across commercial disputes, cheque dishonour matters, MSME payment recovery, RERA complaints, arbitration, and civil/property disputes. Advice is provided in Hindi and English, with an emphasis on clear communication and document-led strategy.

This website provides general information about the chambers and legal topics. It is not intended as advertising, solicitation, or legal advice.
