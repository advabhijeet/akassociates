RERA & Property Matters

General information on builder disputes, delayed possession, refund claims, RERA remedies across Bihar, UP and Delhi NCR, title disputes, partition suits, injunctions, declarations, and other civil property remedies.

Matter Flow

Project & Agreement Review

Builder-buyer agreements, allotment letters, payment receipts, possession timelines, and project details are reviewed.

Forum Assessment

The appropriate route may involve the RERA authority for Bihar, UP or Delhi NCR, civil court, consumer forum, or another remedy depending on facts and relief sought.

Property Documents

For civil disputes, title deeds, mutation records, khatiyan, rent receipts, maps, notices, and prior orders may be relevant.

Common Issues

Common issues include delayed possession, cancellation, refund claims, incomplete amenities, title disputes, partition, injunction, declaration, boundary disputes, and specific performance.

This page is for general information only. Remedies differ by facts, documents, limitation, and the available forum.

FAQ

Which RERA authority is relevant?

The relevant RERA authority generally depends on the project location, documents, relief sought, and procedural stage.

What documents are needed for delayed possession or refund?

Builder-buyer agreements, allotment letters, payment receipts, bank statements, possession timelines, project communications, notices, and replies are commonly reviewed.

Are property title disputes handled the same way as RERA disputes?

Not always. RERA and civil property remedies can involve different forums, documents, limitation issues, and relief. The route depends on the facts.
